Creamy Tortellini Soup

Pasta is a staple in my household because I believe strongly in eating your feelings and carbs are the best vehicle for emotions. Soup is also well known to be the best comfort food so bingo bango bongo, we've got ourselves a winner.



This dish is super easy to prepare, can be done with fresh, frozen, or canned ingredients, so it is very versatile, and it's all going to be slopped together in a creamy soupy bowl of soul-soothing goodness anyway so presentation is a joke! It's a great recipe for even the most disaster prone of us home chefs.
