Simple Directions

Notes:

Don't forget! There's tons of flexibility in this recipe so feel free to get wacky with it.

  1. If using fresh produce, be sure to rinse and dry all produce thoroughly and prep to suggestion as listed in ingredients.
  2. Melt butter in large saucepan or soup pot.
  3. Add onion, garlic, carrots, mushroom, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Cook over medium heat for 6 minutes or until vegetables are tender and garlic/seasoning is fragrant.
  4. Add cream of mushroom
  5. Add chicken broth
  6. Add tortellini, cook for 6-7 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent pasta from sticking to bottom
  7. Add spinach, cook until just wilted
  8. Serve and enjoy, top with parmesan as your heart desires!
