Ingredients for Tortellini Soup

  1. Chicken broth or vegetable broth
  2. Cream of chicken or cream of mushroom
  3. One clove of garlic
  4. Spinach, fresh or frozen
  5. Carrots, diced or in slices
  6. Celery, thinly sliced
  7. Mushrooms, quartered
  8. One onion, thinly sliced

I'm personally pretty loosey goosey with my ingredients. Sometimes I'll throw tomatoes in there, or potatoes, sometimes onions are my enemy. Sometimes I can't stand the smell of celery. Sometimes I haven't been to the grocery store in a month and all my mushrooms have returned to the earth.
